## SEO Audit Framework

### Level 1: Quick Score (Automated)
Run on every new lead's website:
- PageSpeed Insights score (mobile + desktop)
- Mobile-friendliness check
- SSL certificate status
- Meta tags completeness
- Schema markup presence
- Google Business Profile status
- Estimated monthly organic traffic

### Level 2: Full Audit (On request)
- Complete technical crawl (broken links, redirects, duplicate content)
- On-page optimization analysis (titles, descriptions, headers, images)
- Content quality assessment
- Backlink profile review
- Local SEO checklist (50+ items)
- Competitor comparison (top 3 local competitors)
- Actionable recommendations with priority ranking

### Level 3: Strategic Plan (For clients)
- 90-day SEO roadmap
- Content calendar (12 weeks)
- Link building strategy
- Google Business Profile optimization plan
- Monthly reporting template
- KPI targets and milestones
